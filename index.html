Things you should know about kids

  1. Children are rational: Children are nuts. Don't try to reason with them.
  2. Children are dumb: Children are very clever. Because they still ask questions that we adults have given up asking.
  3. Children want you to be their friend: They don't. They want you to be their parent.
  4. Children take any notice of what you say: They don't. They only take notice of what you do.
  5. Children's greatest fear is that you will die: It isn't. Their greatest fear is that they are not good enough to be loved.
